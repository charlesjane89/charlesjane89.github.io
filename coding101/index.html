How I can help

As Nutrition and Lifestyle Coach, I will guide and walk with you on your journey to health and well-being. I believe in a balanced and individual approach for myself and my clients, with freedom to choose what I want to eat with a bit of discipline thrown in. Whatever your reason for wanting a healthier life and wherever you are starting from, I believe 100% that I can help.

See a list of services i offer below

One to One Consultation

The One to One process is where I work individually with the client to develop an Individual Evidence based Nutrition, Lifestyle and Supplement plan. We'll work together together to develop steps that will lead to achievement of the clients goals for example, more energy, weight loss, blood sugar balance, balancing hormones etc. There are a number of packages available from a single Consultation through to a multiple consultations depending on the needs of the client. Check out Prices and Plans if you feel this might suit and give me a call to have a chat.

  • Dietry Analysis
  • Recipes & Tips
  • Meal Planning
  • Additional Support

Small Groups (2-6)

Group Coaching is ideal for a group of friends, work colleagues or family - people who are known to one another and have the same goals. Its a great way to spread the cost and utilise the extra support that the group brings. Included - goal setting, action plans, recipes, cooking demos. Areas we can work on could be:

  • Weight loss
  • Improve energy
  • Manage stress
  • Change eating habits
